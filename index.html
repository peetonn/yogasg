About me

Practicing Yoga since 2009.

My Yoga practice

Yoga for Everyone!

My teaching practice focuses on classical Hatha Yoga suitable for any level of proficiency, from beginners to advanced practitioners. My classes include theory and practice on many aspects: yoga therapy, injury prevention, muscle and joint work, stretches, bends, twists and inversions.

Next

Personalized Approach

The individual abilities and current physical preparation of my students are of primary importance to me. Therefore, I tailor the sequence of asanas to these specifics, harmoniously developing my students’ practice and moving towards more advanced poses. By paying attention to each student, I help them correctly align the poses (asanas), regulate their breathing, and explain the principle of the body’s work in each position and all its subtle aspects. Such an approach allows deepening knowledge of both physiological and psychological aspects.

Next

Theory of Asanas and Pranayama

Special importance is given to breathing. In my classes, I add theory and practice of pranayama techniques that strengthen the respiratory muscles and explain the benefits of proper breathing, which gives rejuvenation and longevity. My classes aim to develop a full and conscious understanding of the interconnected processes that occur in the body at physical, mental, and spiritual levels. I am always open and happy to answer questions and requests from my students, whether it is advanced asana practice, meditative techniques, or related practices such as Reiki, Qigong, or advice on healthy eating (Ayurveda).
